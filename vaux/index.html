FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do I need any technical skills to use Vaux? ▾
None at all. If you can type a text message, you can build your Vaux website. Our editor is point-and-click, and we have a full library of HVAC-specific templates to start from.
How long does it take to go live? ▾
Most HVAC companies are live within 30–60 minutes of signing up. You customize your content, hit publish, and your site is immediately live on a vaux.site subdomain. Connecting your own domain takes an extra 24–48 hours for DNS propagation.
Can I use my own domain name? ▾
Yes. Pro and Elite plans support custom domains (e.g., arcticairhvac.com). Elite includes the domain registration for free. On the Starter plan, you get a free vaux.site subdomain.
What happens after the 14-day trial? ▾
After 14 days, your site stays live and you're prompted to add a payment method. If you choose not to subscribe, your site goes offline but your data is saved for 60 days. No automatic charges ever without your explicit consent.
Can I switch plans later? ▾
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately and you're charged the prorated difference. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is the booking calendar connected to my schedule? ▾
Yes. On Pro and Elite plans, the online booking calendar syncs with Google Calendar and sends automatic email and SMS confirmations to both you and your customer. You can set your availability, service durations, and buffer times.
Do you offer refunds? ▾
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If Vaux isn't working for your business, just email us within 30 days of your first payment and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.
