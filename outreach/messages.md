# Outreach Message Templates

Use these as starting points — always personalize the business name and one
specific detail if you can (a review you read, their specialty, etc.). Never
send the exact same generic text to everyone; small personalization dramatically
improves reply rates.

---

## 1. First message — Facebook Messenger / Instagram DM (best option)

> Hey! I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website yet, so I actually
> went ahead and built you a free sample one to show what it could look like:
> [LIVE DEMO LINK]
>
> No obligation at all — just wanted you to see it. If you like it, I can get
> it fully live for you. Let me know what you think!

**Why this works:** leads with proof, not a pitch. Zero pressure. The demo
does the selling for you.

---

## 2. First message — Email (if no Facebook/Instagram found)

**Subject:** A free website mockup for [Business Name]

> Hi, my name is [Your Name]. I build websites for local service businesses,
> and I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have one yet — so I put together a
> free sample site for you to look at, no strings attached:
>
> [LIVE DEMO LINK]
>
> If you like it, I'd be happy to get it fully published and set up for you.
> If not, no worries at all — just thought it was worth showing you. Let me
> know if you have any questions!
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]

---

## 3. Follow-up (if no response after 3-4 days)

> Hey, just following up in case you missed this — here's that free website
> preview I built for [Business Name]: [LIVE DEMO LINK]. Totally no
> obligation, just let me know if you'd like it published!

Send **one** follow-up like this. If still no response, move on to the next
lead — don't send more than 2 total messages to the same business.

---

## 4. When they say "yes, I want it"

> Awesome! Here's how it works:
> - Final price: **$[PRICE]** for the complete site, hosted and set up for you
> - Once payment is received, I'll remove the "demo" preview banner, connect
>   your real contact info, and it'll be live as your official website within
>   [X hours/days]
> - Payment link: [PAYMENT LINK] *(see invoice-template.md)*
>
> Let me know if you have any questions before we get started!

---

## 5. When they ask "how much / what's the price"

> Sites like this typically run $300-$600 depending on what you need, but for
> your first site I can do it for **$[YOUR INTRO PRICE]** — that includes
> [list what's included: hosting, mobile-friendly design, contact form, etc.]

**Pricing guidance:** For your very first few clients, price low ($150-$250)
to build reviews/testimonials fast. Once you have 2-3 happy clients and real
testimonials, raise prices toward $400-$800 per site — still a great deal for
the business owner, and much better for you.

---

## 6. If they ask "why should I trust you" / "who are you"

> Totally fair question! I specialize in building fast, professional websites
> for local service businesses like HVAC and plumbing companies. I work
> remotely, which is how I can offer such a fair price — but the work speaks
> for itself, which is exactly why I built you a real working sample instead
> of just describing it. Happy to answer anything else!

Avoid volunteering your exact location unless asked directly — if asked, be
honest, but frame it around the value/price advantage rather than leading
with it.
